Pulau Langkawi

Far Far Away Island

Langkawi is an archipelago located off the northwest coast of Peninsular Malaysia in the state of Kedah. It is made up of 99 islands and is known for its beautiful beaches, clear blue waters, and lush forests. Visitors to Langkawi can enjoy a variety of activities, such as swimming and sunbathing on the island's sandy beaches, hiking through the island's forests and mangroves, and exploring the island's cultural attractions, such as the Langkawi Sky Bridge and the Atma Alam Batik Art Village. Other must-see attractions in Langkawi include the Langkawi Cable Car and the Langkawi Bird Paradise. There are a number of accommodation options available in Langkawi, including hotels, resorts, and vacation rentals. Some popular options include the Four Seasons Resort Langkawi, the Datai Langkawi, and the Meritus Pelangi Beach Resort & Spa. Langkawi is also home to a variety of dining options, ranging from local seafood restaurants to international cafes and bars. Some popular eating places include the Langkawi Food Court and the Tanjung Rhu Beach Restaurant. It is important for visitors to Langkawi to be mindful of local cultural considerations, such as dressing modestly and respecting local customs and traditions.
